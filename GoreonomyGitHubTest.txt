Goreonomy: The Index Thesaurus-developed by Lizzy Walker, Associate Professor, Metadata & Digital Initiatives Librarian, Wichita State University Libraries

The Goreonomy is currently being updated. We are aware that LCSH contains problematic language. That is why the Goreonomy does not solely rely on the established subject headings for our tags. As terminology is updated by various Subject Authority Cooperative Program (SACO) funnels that are working to correct and update headings, we will ensure our own tags are kept up to date. Please contact us if you find a tag in the index problematic and we will find a solution.
